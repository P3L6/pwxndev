---
title: Taco bell sauce
meal: sauce
---

* 1 cup mayo
* 2 t minced jalapenos (optional)
* 1 t sugar
* 2 t paprika
* 2 t cumin
* 1 t cayenne pepper, or a bit more
* 1/2 t garlic powder
* 1/8 tsp salt
* 8 tsp jalapeno juice from jarred jalapenos (add last)

mix well, let chill, mix again after an hour to two.
