| Biscuits | Gravy | Ingredients |
|:-:|:-:|-|
2¼ C | | bisquick
⅔ C | 2-3 C | milk
&nbsp; | 3 Tbsp | grease
&nbsp; | 3 Tbsp | flour

## Directions

1. For drop biscuits:
   1. Combine bisquick and ⅔C milk.
   2. Spoon out onto baking sheet.
   3. Bake 8-10 mins at 450.
2. For gravy:
   1. Make a roux.
   2. Slowly add milk, stirring constantly.
   3. Add sausage from grease.
