|| Ingredients |
|-:|-|
1 lb   | potatoes
½ Tbsp | salt
½ C    | half and half
1+     | clove garlic
some   | parmesan (optional)

## Directions

1. Quarter and boil potatoes.
2. Mash, stirring in remaining ingredients.

### Appendix

* Can substitute half and half with milk and butter.
